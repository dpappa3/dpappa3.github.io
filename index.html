6 Ways to be an Eco-Friendly College Student

"The greatest threat to our planet is the belief that someone else will save it." -Robert Swan

GO GREEN AND REDUCE YOUR CARBON FOOTPRINT!

Tip 1

Recycle!

Think about how much paper you use throughout the semester or how many water bottles you accumulate! One way to combat these issues is by disposing of them properly. Utulize the recycling bins around campus and in dorm halls. Another way may be to take notes on your laptop or to purchase a refillable water bottle.

Tip 2

Wash Cups and Plates

As a busy college student, it can be hard to avoid the temptation of buying disposable paper or platic plates, cups, and utensils. Sure it's convenient, but it adds up to a lot of waste and MONEY!'

Tip 3

Purchase Used Textbooks

Not only will this discourage new printing, but it's also super cost efficient for you and the environment!

Tip 4

Walk, Bike, or Take Public Transit

If you live on campus, then this one is pretty easy. If you live off campus, utulize the TigerTrails bus route with the app called TransLoc Rider.If you do have to drive, take some friends with you.

Tip 5

Double Check your Documents

When printing, save misprints, bad copies and mistakes by always double checking before you click the print button!

Tip 6

Reduce your Water Usage

This can be done by simply turning off the faucet while brushing your teeth or cutting some of your morning/nightly jam sessions in the shower short.
